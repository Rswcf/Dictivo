## Local transcription engine

Dictivo embeds and links against [`whisper.cpp`](https://github.com/ggml-org/whisper.cpp) by Georgi Gerganov, distributed under the **MIT License**. The downloaded model weights (GGML quantized Whisper models) are derived from OpenAI's Whisper, distributed under the **MIT License**.

The benchmark audio sample shipped at `apps/desktop/src-tauri/resources/benchmark-5s.wav` was generated by the Dictivo team specifically for hardware benchmarking and is licensed under the same FSL-1.1-MIT as the rest of Dictivo.
