Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't Dictivo a subscription?

Because dictation is a tool, not a service. Once it's installed it works offline forever — there's no cloud cost we'd be hiding behind a monthly fee. We charge once for the version you get today, and we charge a small annual fee if you want to keep receiving new versions. If you don't want to renew, the app you have keeps working.

What happens after 12 months if I don't renew?

Nothing breaks. Your Dictivo continues to work — every feature, every model you have downloaded, offline, forever. We will never remotely disable a paid Dictivo build. What stops is the delivery of new versions and new transcription models. If you'd like to receive them again, you can renew for $24/year at any time, and your update window picks up from the moment you renew.

What does my $49 include?
  • A perpetual license to the version of Dictivo you download today
  • Every update we ship in the next 12 months — new models, new languages, new features
  • Use on up to 2 of your personal devices
  • 14-day full refund
Is there a free version?

Yes — the free tier includes the tiny Whisper model, unlimited dictation, and the global hotkey. It's enough to try the experience and decide whether the larger models and the Pro features are worth $49.

Can I move my license to a new computer?

Yes. Sign in or paste your license key on the new machine. Dictivo allows up to 2 active machines at once; activating a third deactivates the least-recently-used.

What about Windows?

Windows support is on the roadmap for version 1.1, a few months after the macOS launch. Drop your email below to be notified when it ships.

What if Dictivo, the company, disappears?

Two protections: (1) Perpetual fallback — the version you have installed runs offline forever, with no server check. (2) License is offline-verified — your activation is cached locally, so the app never depends on our license server staying up.

Will you ever switch to subscription?

No. If we ever launch a separate Dictivo Cloud product that adds optional cloud features, it will be a separate purchase. Buying Dictivo today entitles you to Dictivo on your laptop, forever, at the terms above. That's in the EULA in writing.

Refund policy?

14 days, full amount, no questions. Email hello@dictivo.app with your order number.
