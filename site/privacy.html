Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-14

Dictivo ("we", "us") is designed so that the only data we ever receive from you is the data you knowingly send us when you purchase the product. This page describes what we collect, when, and why — in plain language.

1. What stays on your device

Your audio recordings, transcripts, dictionary entries, snippets, hotkey settings, and any text generated by Dictivo are stored only on the computer where you installed Dictivo. They are never transmitted to Dictivo or to any third party.

2. What happens when Dictivo checks for updates

Every 24 hours (and once at launch), the desktop app sends a single HTTP request to https://dictivo.app/latest.json. The request includes:

  • Your operating system and architecture (so we send the right installer)
  • Your current Dictivo version
  • Your IP address (automatically attached by the network — we do not log it)

No identifiers, no installation UUID, no usage data. You can disable automatic update checks in Settings → License & Updates.

3. What happens when you activate a license

When you paste your license key into Dictivo, the app calls api.lemonsqueezy.com once to validate it and get your purchase details (your name, email, and purchase date as you provided to Lemon Squeezy at checkout). This information is cached locally in ~/Library/Application Support/Dictivo/license.json so the app never needs to contact our servers again.

4. What Lemon Squeezy receives when you purchase

Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our Merchant of Record. When you check out, they receive your name, email, billing address, payment details, and tax information as required by law. Their privacy policy applies to that data: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy .

From Lemon Squeezy we then receive only:

  • Your name (as you provided)
  • Your email (so we can send your license key)
  • A Lemon Squeezy order ID
  • Your country (for tax records)

We retain this for the lifetime of your license plus 7 years (tax-record retention requirement).

5. What this website (dictivo.app) does

This marketing site is a static HTML page hosted on Cloudflare Pages. It loads no analytics scripts, sets no cookies, and runs no JavaScript that would identify you. Cloudflare's edge logs may record your IP address and user agent for operational purposes; we do not export or retain these logs.

6. Subprocessors

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and license email delivery
  • Cloudflare — site hosting and CDN
  • GitHub — release artifact hosting (installer downloads)

7. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You may, at any time:

  • Request a copy of all data we hold tied to your email address
  • Request deletion of that data, except where retention is required for tax compliance
  • Cancel any active subscription directly through the Lemon Squeezy customer portal linked in your purchase receipt

Email hello@dictivo.app for any of the above. We respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the "Last updated" date at the top changes. For any material change, we will state the change on this page and on the home page for at least 30 days.
