## FAQ

### Why isn't Dictivo a subscription like Wispr Flow?

Because dictation is a tool, not a service. Once it's installed, it works offline forever — there's no cloud cost we'd be hiding behind a monthly fee. We charge once for the version you get today, and we charge a small annual fee if you want to keep getting *new* versions. If you don't want to renew, the app you have keeps working. Forever. We mean it.

### What exactly does my $49 include?

- A perpetual license to the version of Dictivo you download today, plus every update we ship in the next 12 months.
- Use on up to 2 of your personal devices, on either macOS or Windows.
- Future macOS, Windows, and accessibility improvements that ship as 1.x updates inside your window.
- New transcription models we ship as 1.x updates, including upgrades to the underlying Whisper engine.
- 14-day full refund. Email us once and your money is back.

### What happens after 12 months if I don't renew?

Nothing breaks. Your Dictivo continues to work — every feature, every model you have downloaded, offline, forever. We will never remotely disable a paid Dictivo build.

What *does* happen: we stop sending you new versions and new models. If you'd like to receive them again, you can renew for $24/year at any time, and your update window picks up from the moment you renew.

### Why renew at all?

Because we'll keep shipping. New Whisper-family models come out a few times a year and they keep getting better. We'll integrate them as soon as they're stable. There will be UI improvements, new languages, performance work on Apple Silicon and Snapdragon X. If any of that sounds worth $24, renew. If not, your current Dictivo is yours to keep.

### Is there a free version?

Yes — the free tier of Dictivo includes the `tiny` model, unlimited dictation, and the hotkey. It's enough to try the experience and decide whether the larger models and Pro features are worth $49.

### Can I move my license to a new computer?

Yes. Sign in or paste your activation token on the new machine. The license remembers up to 2 active machines; activating a third deactivates the least-recently-used one automatically.

### Is the source visible?

The transcription engine ([whisper.cpp](https://github.com/ggml-org/whisper.cpp)) is open source. The Dictivo client is closed source — we want to be honest about that. We sign every release with both Apple's Developer ID and an updater signature you can verify; the public key is published [here](TBD).

### Do you have a student discount?

Yes — `STUDENT` at checkout gets you 30% off with a `.edu` (or equivalent) email. Same license, same updates, just cheaper.

### Team licenses?

Email us at hello@dictivo.app. We sell packs of 5 / 10 / 25 seats at a per-seat discount, managed by a single admin token.

### I bought Dictivo, where's my license?

Check your inbox (and spam) for an email from `hello@dictivo.app` containing an **Activate Dictivo** link. Click it once with Dictivo running. If you've lost it, paste your Lemon Squeezy order number into Settings → License → Recover.

### What if Dictivo, the company, disappears?

Two protections:
1. **Perpetual fallback.** The version you have installed runs offline forever, with no server check, no license phone-home that would fail if our domain expired.
2. **Escrow.** The Ed25519 license-verification public key is baked into your binary. The license server's purpose is only to *issue* new tokens. If we vanish, no new tokens get issued, but every previously issued license keeps working.

### Why $49 and not $99 / $19?

Because $49 is roughly what we'd want to pay for a tool we use daily. Higher than that and it feels like buying enterprise software; lower and we can't ship updates for a decade. Renewals at $24 are deliberately well below the cost of one month of Wispr Flow.

### Where is my money going?

Roughly: 30% to Lemon Squeezy fees + VAT, 30% to engineering and design time, 20% to code-signing and infrastructure, 20% to keeping the company alive across the gaps when sales are slow. We don't take VC money. There is no "we have to grow 30% a year or die" pressure pushing us toward a subscription rug-pull.

### Will you ever switch to subscription?

Anyone you give your money to today is the answer to this question. So: **no.** If we ever launch a separate Dictivo Cloud product that adds optional cloud features (cross-device sync, GPT-4-class post-processing), it will be a *separate* purchase. Buying Dictivo today entitles you to Dictivo on your laptop, forever, at the terms above. That's in the EULA in writing, and we'd rather close the company than break it.

### Refund policy?

14 days, full amount, no questions. Email `support@dictivo.app` with your order number. We don't make you click through 5 dialogs.
